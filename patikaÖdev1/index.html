Yaşar Üniversitesi Blockchain Topluluğu

YU Blockchain and Finance Community, is a student community established by Yaşar University students in 2022. We aim to be an efficient, working and productive student community by bringing students together from different departments of our university and prepare them to trending blockchain technology by presenting them lessons both in financial and software parts of this new technologies. Our mission is to establish strong connections with other universities on an international scale, to carry out professional projects in the field of technology, to take part in both national and local competitions and reveal the professional leaders of the future.
